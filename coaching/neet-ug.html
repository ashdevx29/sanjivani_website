NEET-UG Exam Overview (2026)

  • Mode of Exam: Offline (Pen & Paper based)
  • Subjects: Physics, Chemistry & Biology (Zoology + Botany)
  • Total Questions: 200 (Students attempt 180)
  • Marking Scheme: +4 for correct answer, -1 for incorrect answer
  • Duration: 3 Hours 20 Minutes
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry & Biology (50% for General, 40% for Reserved categories)
